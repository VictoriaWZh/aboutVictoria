ABOUT ME

My name is Victoria Zhang. I am 14 years old, and I am currently attending 8th grade at Eastside Preparatory School. My hometown is Denton, Texas, which I don't have any memories of. My favorite place I have ever visited is Banff National Park in Alberta, Canada because of all the breathtaking views, flourishing wildlife, and serene environment: the medley of mountains, lakes, wildflowers, and other scenery make Banff simply spectacular.

My favorite season would be fall, since my birthday (September 22) generally falls on the first day (Fall Equinox). I treasure the cozy vibes and the remarkable spectrum of leaves turning colors, falling off trees and covering the ground with a gorgeous pattern of reds, oranges, and yellows. My favorite book is "The Glass Castle" by Jeanette Walls, and although it is an older classic, the biography and story intrigues me.

I can speak English and Chinese both fluently, and a bit of Spanish. My hobbies would include reading, writing (short stories), coding in Python/Java, and Equestrianism. I am an avid equestrian: I ride horses and am a D2 Eventer in the USPC, working towards becoming D3 certified. Related to this sport I'm passionate about, comes my favorite quote: "Head up, heels down.", which explains such a simple four-word riding tip, but also as a powerful attitude towards life in general.
